Hello! Welcome to BRIDGE

Have friends and family in different time zones you'd like to call together? Our site makes it easy to coordinate by converting time zones and finding overlapping availabilities in just four simple steps:

  1. Choose a base city to which the other cities’ time zones will be converted.
  2. Select two additional cities to compare for overlapping times.
  3. Optionally, input each person’s availability in their respective time zones.
  4. View city cards and a graph to identify overlapping times for your call.
